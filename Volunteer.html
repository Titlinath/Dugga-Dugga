Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to volunteer?

Volunteers must be at least 18 years old. Minors can participate only with parental consent in supervised roles.

Do volunteers receive any allowance or food?

We provide meal coupons and travel reimbursements for shifts longer than 4 hours. Volunteers also get a welcome kit.

Can I volunteer for only a few hours?

Yes! We offer flexible shifts (2-4 hours) and part-time options. Select 'Flexible Hours' in the signup form.

What kind of training will I receive?

You’ll attend a 2-hour session covering safety protocols, emergency response, and role-specific tasks. Training is mandatory.

Safety & Code of Conduct

Always Wear Your Badge

Your volunteer ID must be visible at all times for identification and authority.

Report Suspicious Activity

Immediately alert police or your coordinator about unattended bags, conflicts, or safety hazards.

Respect All Visitors

Be courteous, non-discriminatory, and patient. Assist without bias regardless of gender, age, or background.

Stay Within Your Role

Do not attempt medical procedures or law enforcement actions beyond your training. Know your limits!
