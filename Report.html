Safety Suggestions

Stay Calm

Take deep breaths and try to remain calm. Panic can cloud your judgment.

Seek Help

Approach police, volunteers, or nearby families for assistance.

Collect Evidence

If safe, take photos or videos of the incident or people involved.

Remember, you're not alone. We're here to support you.

Chat Support & FAQs

Our virtual assistant can help guide you through the reporting process.

Where can I go if I feel unsafe?
Look for police booths, women's safety booths (pink tents), or volunteer help desks. They are located near all major pandals.
How do I report anonymously?
Check the "Submit anonymously" option on the report form. We won't collect any personal information.
What information should I provide?
Provide as much detail as possible: time, location, description of people involved, and any evidence you have.
How long does it take to respond?
Emergency reports are prioritized and responded to within 15 minutes. Non-emergency reports may take up to 24 hours.
